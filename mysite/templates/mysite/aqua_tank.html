Aqua Tank

This project got funded from NEWGEN IEDC(Innovation and Entrepreneurship Division Cell of Government of India

AQUA TANK brings in automation of tanks containing liquids ranging from water to any inflammable liquid like gasoline or toxic chemicals. With the help of this invention a ground breaking step is taken towards monitoring and operating industrial and household tanks. We are looking at a future full of smart devices and smart cities. In the quest of making gadgets smarter we bring forward our invention i.e. AQUA TANK. Fluid tank industry is quite stagnant over the years. With our device these tanks will get smarter and also remove a ton load of burden from users hence making household and industries smarter.

Features of AQUA TANK are:


  • Non-physical contact mechanism
  • Saves water by preventing overflows rather than detecting overflows
  • Easy to install
  • Cost equivalent to overflow alarms but provides way more functionality
  • Reduces effort of refilling tanks on constant basis

Anatomy of AQUA TANK

The major technical components are:

  • Arduino Uno
  • Relay
  • Atmega 328p
  • Ultrasonic Wave Generator
  • 9V Battery
